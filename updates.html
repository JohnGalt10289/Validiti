Data Analysis

Data collection and testing

We have already collected data sets for internal testing, which has indicated true randomness in our data. We will now seek external testing and validation. We will collect additional samples, using different techniques for each sample set, analyse them and identify the random values. We will submit these datasets to cryptography experts in academic, government, and private institutions. We anticipate this challenge will be accepted by smaller institutions before scaling to larger, more esteemed organizations.

Defining the Victory

No matter the outcome of the challenge, a valuable discovery awaits

While our intial testing matched our belief that our data is truly random, establishing a predictable, mathematical relationship between the data points will provide a very valuable understanding of human choice. This connection would prove to be very valuable to marketing efforts. If the outcome reveals true randomness, then cryptography and other areas of research will enter into a whole new era. Perhaps we will end up with degrees of both.
